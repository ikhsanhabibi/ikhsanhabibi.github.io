MAKE THE PLASTICS AND PAPERS BECOME THE EARTH FRIENDS!

About

Plastics and papers are the most important materials for daily activities. But we have damaged the environment! Why? Acres of forest have been destroyed to make papers. Or we have used a kind of material which is not biodegradable in the soil such as plastic.

We are interested in discussing about the alternative materials to substitute the synthetic plastics which are dangerous if they are not recycled and reused on proper ways. Meanwhile, it is time to reduce the use of papers too. Do you know that papers are made of wood? Can you imagine that someday we live without trees? Very hot!

We created a project of making bioplastics and papers which are friendly for our environment. Now, please enjoy it...

The project's essential problem, question or issue:
How can we use the materials friendly with our environment?
