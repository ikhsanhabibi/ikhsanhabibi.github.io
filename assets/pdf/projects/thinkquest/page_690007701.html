Environment is Increasingly Damaged

Humans are the components of the ecosystem that can be potentially as a savior and destroyer of ecosystems. Examples of human activities that damage the ecosystem is deforestation and the construction of settlements without regard to environmental aspects.

 Sometimes people create an ecosystem that had good aims. But over time, it even can be turned around because of his own human ego. For example, the wetland ecosystems contained locusts, farmers will eradicate the pests which indirectly destroy the locust predators. This is tantamount disrupt the food chain.

 Basically, the ecosystem still can repair itself (self purification) to achieve balance again within a certain timeframe. A minute or longer, depending on the level of damage. Unfortunately, humans often have even more damaging ecosystems that are trying to improve himself. So, one might say that the components of the most potent destroyer of ecosystems are human.

 Why humans? Are not many natural disasters which are very damaging to the ecosystem? The answer is easy, 'Because most of the disaster was man himself who created'.

 Global Warming or Global Warming, the hot topics discussed was also one of the result of human activities. "Most of the increase in global average temperatures since the mid-20th century most likely caused by increased concentrations of greenhouse gases caused by human activities through the greenhouse effect," as the conclusion of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC).

 The occurrence of Global Warming can also destroy the ecosystem. Just imagine if the earth's temperature has increased so that the animals tend to migrate toward the poles or up mountains, plants will change the direction of its growth, finding new areas because of the length of habitat becomes too warm. However, due to human development prevents this displacement, the species that migrate unobstructed. Some types of species that are not able to quickly move may be destroyed. For humans, animal migration can be a big problem with the spread of various diseases and the spread of viruses that carried the animal. For example, increasing the occurrence of dengue fever because mosquitoes have to breed a new ecosystem.

 Of course we do not want the earth destroyed ecosystems. As inhabitants of the earth the most perfect human being should be able to come take care of this earth. Things to do is to anticipate our daily activities. Activities that if damage to the environment should be reduced and eliminated. While the tradition of preserving the environment should digencarkan. Start from small things like planting trees to other activities. Although simple, at least we took part in the effort to 'cure' ecosystem. Not just for the sake of the ecosystem and the physical universe, but also for life in the future.
