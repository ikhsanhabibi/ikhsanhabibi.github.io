How to Make Bioplastic From Potato

  1. Wash, peel and grate the potatoes.
  2. Add 100 ml water.
  3. Grind the potato.
  4. Pour the liquid through a tea strainer to beaker glass.
  5. Repeat the step 1 to 4.
  6. Wait for 5 minutes, after that the starch settles out.
  7. The liquid can be decanted. We just need the starch to make bioplastic.
  8. Now starch is left in the beaker glass.
  9. Add 100 ml water to rinse the starch. Stir well.
  10. Again, the liquid can be decanted off.
  11. Now we get the wet and clean starch.
  12. Dry it in an oven and we get white powder.
  13. Place 25 ml powder to beaker glass, add 25 ml water.
  14. Pour 3 ml Hidrochloric acid 0,1 M and stir.
  15.  Add 2 ml of Glycerol, stir until soluble.
  16. Heat the mixture with a bunsen burner for 15 minutes.
  17. Add 0,1 M NaOH and stir.
  18. Pour the mixture into the petri dish and spread it out.
  19. You can add food coloring to make it more interesting.
  20. Bake in the oven.
  21. Well done. You get your own bioplastic!
