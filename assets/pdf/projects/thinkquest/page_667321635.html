Fantastic Fact about Plastic!

  • It takes a very long time for plastic to break down

    An average 323 plastic bags are taken into our homes every year and it takes 500 years to decay when sent to landfill. It also takes about 450 years just for one plastic bottle to break down in the ground.

  • Recycle is not occur maximum in many country

    9.1 billion plastic bottles were disposed of in 2002 with only 360 million of them being recycled. Only 2.5% of plastic bottles are presently recycled in Europe.

  • The profit of recycle

    Recycling a single plastic bottle can conserve enough energy to light a 60W bulb for up to 6 hours.

  • How many plastic that we use? Actually, i cant imagine that

    In Britain we use about 275,000 tonnes of plastic bottles in our homes every year- that is about 15 million bottles every day. About 80% waste in the ocean come from the land, and almost 90% is plastic.

  • The Influence to the universe

    Each year, plastic has been 'killed' up to 1 million seabirds, 100,000 marine mammals and fish were countless. Many turtles in the thousand islands (Jakarta-Indonesia) who died from eating plastic because they thought plastics in water are jellyfish, their favorite food.
