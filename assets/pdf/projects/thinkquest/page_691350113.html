What is Bamboo Paper?

Chinese People were produced bamboo paper more than 1500 years ago. What is the benefits of bamboo paper?

  • Bamboo paper also can be recycled . Just like paper made ​​from wood pulp , bamboo paper also can be recycled to reduce impact on the environment and reduce our dependence on wood .
  • Bamboo can thrive in an arid land where rain forests cut down and burned . Bamboo is one of the few plants that can grow easily in a variety of environmental conditions . Bamboo also can grow in the mountains and on steep slopes .
  • Bamboo is a renewable resource that can be quickly . Bamboo is a species of the fastest growing plant on earth that can grow to more than one meter a day . While other trees require decades to recover from harvesting , bamboo reaches maturity in just 3 to 5 years or less and when cut, the stem is left on the ground can grow into new shoots and will grow back into the bamboo tree .
  • Bamboo helps reduce soil erosion . The roots of bamboo plants that branch out from the stalk can help to keep the soil from erosion and retain soil moisture . It also helps to precipitate the mud in the river that cam affecting aquatic life .

Bamboo plants provide many benefits to nature and grow in the earth . It also can be a good quality paper . Why do not we develop the production of paper from bamboo ?
